What Pulse Does

Your AI CTO assistant

Pulse analyzes every signal from your dev stack and tells you exactly what to fix — before it becomes a crisis.

🏥
Engineering Health Score
A single 0–100 score updated weekly across 6 dimensions: velocity, quality, reliability, collaboration, burnout risk, and predictability.
🔍
Bottleneck Detection
Identifies exactly which engineer, service, or process is slowing your team down — with specific, actionable recommendations.
🔮
Sprint Predictions
Predicts sprint completion probability based on historical velocity. Know by Wednesday if Friday's deadline is at risk.
🚨
Burnout Signals
Detects multi-signal burnout risk per engineer — after-hours commits, on-call load, review drop-off — before it becomes attrition.
📊
DORA Metrics
Track all 4 DORA metrics automatically: deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, and MTTR. Benchmarked against industry data.
🤖
AI Brief Generator
Every Monday at 7 AM, Pulse sends a human-readable AI brief to Slack. What broke, why, who's at risk, what to do this week.
